Outdoors

My Story

I've always been a nature gal - what started off as playing in the sand and climbing trees has blossomed into a love of hiking, climbing, running, and overall just spending time outdoors. Each destination I've lived in has allowed me to explore new environments and nurture a deeper appreciation of nature.

Monterey, CA

I spent the first eight years of my life in beautiful, sunny Monterey. My time was spent running on the beach, climbing up trees, and hopping between boulders

Half Moon Bay, CA

From the ages of 8 to 16, I lived in the quaint coastal town of Half Moon Bay. Though I often took the beach for granted, I spent a lot of time watching sunsets through the cypresses and on the coastal trail. I also explored other parts of the bay area while living here.

Ljubljana, Slovenia

A two-year stint in the capital of Slovenia during high school opened up a love of alpine environments. Situated beneath the Julian Alps, I was able to explore beautiful mountain ranges and alpine lakes.

Davis, CA

Though flatland, I have explore the few hiking trails in the vicinity of Davis. Due to the lack of mountainous terrain locally, my time in Davis has also led me to explore Lake Tahoe and other parts of the Sierras.

Seattle, WA

Having spent two summers in Seattle and moving there post-grad, I have dipped my toes into the beautiful alpine trails. The lakes, waterfalls, and mountains remind me of Slovenia in many ways.
